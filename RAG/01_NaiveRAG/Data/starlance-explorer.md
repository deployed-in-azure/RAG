# Starlance Explorer

## Overview
The Starlance Explorer is a ship used for science missions and long‑range surveys.

## Specifications
- Top Speed: Warp 3.0
- Fuel: Tritium‑Fusion Cells
- Seats: 58 passengers
- Artificial Gravity: Yes
- Faster‑Than‑Light Travel: Long‑Range Warp Drive

## Equipment
- Space scanners
- Survey drones
- Tools for studying planets and stars

## Notes
The Starlance is built for exploration, not comfort. It’s often used on long science trips.
