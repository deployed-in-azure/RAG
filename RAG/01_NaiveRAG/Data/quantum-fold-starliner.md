# Quantum‑Fold Starliner

## Overview
The Quantum‑Fold Starliner is an experimental ship that can “fold” space to travel extremely long distances very quickly.

## Specifications
- Top Speed: Quantum‑Fold Travel (very fast)
- Fuel: Exotic Matter
- Seats: 180 passengers
- Artificial Gravity: Yes
- Faster‑Than‑Light Travel: Quantum‑Fold Drive

## Features
- Comfortable lounges
- Special viewing room for fold jumps

## Notes
The Starliner is incredibly fast, but its fold jumps can feel strange to some passengers.
